Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Introduction

Welcome to The Yurodivian. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our website.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This may include your email address if you sign up for our mailing list, and your name if you choose to provide it. You are not required to provide this information to access our free ebooks.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to send you emails if you subscribe to our mailing list, share updates, resources, or new ebook releases, and respond to enquiries if you contact us. You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every email.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use analytics tools or tracking cookies. We do not monitor your browsing behaviour.

Third-Party Services

If you subscribe to our email list, your information may be processed by our email service provider to deliver emails. These providers are required to handle your data securely and only for email delivery purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, or unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, request correction or deletion of your personal information, and unsubscribe from emails at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details on the Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
