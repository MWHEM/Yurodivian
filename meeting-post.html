Meeting — 14 March 2026

Duration: 1 hour 40 minutes

Members shared their experiences working through the later chapters of Soul Embodiment, with a long open discussion on the practice of surrender. New members joined for the first time.

The session covered the distinction between resistance and acceptance as described in the text, and a number of members brought specific questions from their daily application of the material. The conversation moved into broader territory in the second half — on the relationship between the inner work and how it registers in daily life.
